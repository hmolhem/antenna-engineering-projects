# Key Results

## Design baseline

- Design frequency: **1.120 GHz**
- Theoretical half-wave length: **133.93 mm**
- Tuned free-space length: **122.0 mm**
- Tuned free-space minimum: **-15.52702 dB at 1.1168 GHz**
- Free-space S11 at 1.120 GHz: **-15.48433 dB**

## Body-loading comparison at 1.120 GHz

| Case | Matching-minimum frequency | S11 at target | Radiation efficiency | Total efficiency | Gain |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| 10 mm | 1.0312 GHz | -7.839072 dB | -3.410 dB (45.60%) | -4.190 dB (38.11%) | 0.7515 dBi |
| 5 mm | 0.9792 GHz | -5.903831 dB | -3.837 dB (41.33%) | -5.126 dB (30.72%) | 0.3534 dBi |
| 0.1 mm | 1.3976 GHz | -6.951098 dB | -10.52 dB (8.87%) | -11.50 dB (7.08%) | -5.261 dBi |

## Retuning

- Selected 5 mm retuned length: **106.6 mm**
- Matching minimum: **1.0928 GHz**
- Minimum S11: **-8.152857 dB**
- S11 at 1.120 GHz: **-8.000936 dB**
- The retuning improved target-frequency matching but did not reach the -10 dB criterion.

## Numerical-reliability classification

The 0.1 mm non-monotonic matching-minimum shift was not independently verified through mesh or boundary convergence. It is retained as a fixed-model simulation observation, not a convergence-validated trend. No measured or fabricated validation is claimed.
