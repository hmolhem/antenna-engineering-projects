\section{Dipole Antenna Above the Multilayer \\Body Model}

After the free-space antenna was tuned, the same dipole was placed above the multilayer body model at three different separation distances. Comparing these cases shows how increasing near-field coupling to the lossy body changes the antenna impedance, matching frequency, efficiency, gain, and radiation behavior.
