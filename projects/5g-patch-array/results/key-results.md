| -90 degrees | Approximately 20 degrees toward the phi = 0 half-plane |

The ideal two-element array-factor model predicts a 30-degree scan magnitude for a 90-degree progressive phase at approximately half-wavelength spacing. The CST result includes finite element patterns, mutual coupling, substrate loading, feed geometry, and a finite ground plane.
